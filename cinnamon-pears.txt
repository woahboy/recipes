Ingredients:
 - 1 can pears in heavy syrup
 - 1 bag red hots / cinnamon imperials (about 1 cup)

Directions:
 1. Open can of pears, drain syrup into pan
 2. Heat syrup to almost boiling
 3. Add red hots to syrup, stir constantly until candies dissolve
 4. Pour syrup back into can of pears
 5. Refridgerate 24 hours
